December 26, 2025
Dear Gel,

I hope you see this letter. I am grateful that you have suddenly become somewhat close to me.

I was out with my friends in Toledo. We walked and talked about random things, and we also took pictures with the props they have displayed in Megadome. It was fun. My gang (I call them my gang) played badminton in Bato at that time. Then, one of the gang said, "We should go to Toledo and eat dinner." After dinner, the gang and I—it was not planned—went to Megadome, and we had fun.

I just wanted to tell you that yeah, when you asked, "Do you like me?" Yeah, I do. Not just because you are a beautiful person but also a heartwarming one too. But because you put up with my antics. And by the time I realized it, I had fallen for you. (I should stop getting myself attached to people so easily.)

Can I take you out on a date?

You can directly say yes or no. By doing that,
it will help me clarify what you actually think of me.

Yours truly,

Arc
